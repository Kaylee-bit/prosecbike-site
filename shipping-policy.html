Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. General Information

As a professional B2B manufacturer, we offer flexible shipping solutions to meet the needs of our global partners. All shipments originate from our facility in Shenzhen, China.

2. Shipping Methods

  • Express Courier (Samples & Small Orders): DHL, FedEx, UPS, and TNT. Delivery usually takes 3-7 business days.
  • Air Freight: Suitable for medium-sized bulk orders. Delivery usually takes 5-10 business days.
  • Sea Freight: Recommended for large bulk orders (FCL/LCL). Cost-effective for high-volume shipments.

3. Trade Terms (Incoterms)

We primarily operate under EXW (Ex Works) or FOB (Free on Board) Shenzhen terms. Other terms such as CIF or DDP can be negotiated based on specific order requirements.

4. Lead Times

  • Samples: 3-5 business days after payment.
  • Bulk Orders: 15-25 business days depending on quantity and customization requirements.

5. Customs and Duties

For B2B orders, the buyer is responsible for all import duties, taxes, and customs clearance procedures in the destination country unless otherwise specified in a DDP agreement.
