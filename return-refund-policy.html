Return and Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. B2B Return Policy

Due to the nature of B2B transactions and custom manufacturing, we do not accept returns for "change of mind." We only accept returns or provide replacements for items that are proven to have manufacturing defects or do not meet the agreed-upon specifications.

2. Defective Items

If you receive goods that are damaged or defective, you must notify us within 14 calendar days of receiving the shipment. Please provide photographic or video evidence and a detailed description of the issue.

3. Resolutions

Depending on the situation, we will offer one of the following solutions:

  • Replacement of defective units in your next shipment.
  • Partial or full refund for the defective units.
  • Providing replacement parts and technical support for field repairs.

4. Shipping Costs for Returns

Unless otherwise agreed, the cost of returning goods to our facility for inspection shall be borne by the buyer. We will cover the cost of shipping replacement units back to you.

5. Custom Orders

Orders featuring custom logos, packaging, or custom hardware modifications (ODM) are strictly non-refundable once production has commenced.
