Wholesale FAQ

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Standard Products: We support small trial orders starting from 10-50 units to help you test your local market.
Customized Orders: For Logo printing or custom packaging, the MOQ typically starts from 500 units.

Do you support OEM/ODM customization?

Absolutely. As a professional manufacturer, we provide end-to-end customization including:

  • Silk-screen or Laser-etched Logo.
  • Customized Retail Packaging & Multi-language Manuals.

Can I request samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes, we encourage sample testing. Samples are usually dispatched within 3-5 business days via express courier (DHL/FedEx/UPS) so you can verify the quality firsthand.

What is the standard lead time for mass production?

Sample/Small orders: 3-7 days.
Bulk orders (under 2,000 units): 15-20 days after payment confirmation.
Custom mold/Large ODM projects: Timeline to be discussed based on complexity.

Do your products come with international certifications?

Yes. Our main products are fully compliant with CE, FCC, and RoHS standards. We can provide the necessary test reports and certificates to assist with your local customs clearance and market compliance.

What is your warranty and after-sales policy?

We provide a 12-month quality warranty on all hardware. In the rare event of a manufacturing defect, we offer replacements or credit on your next order to ensure your business remains uninterrupted.
