Warranty Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. Warranty Period

We provide a 12-month standard manufacturer warranty on all smart bike accessories (GPS trackers, alarms, lights, pumps, and locks) starting from the date of receipt of the goods.

2. Coverage

This warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. During the warranty period, we will repair or replace, at no charge, products or parts of a product that prove defective because of improper material or workmanship.

3. Exclusions

The warranty does not cover issues caused by:

  • Improper installation or use not in accordance with the product manual.
  • Unauthorized modification or repair.
  • Accidental damage, abuse, or neglect.
  • Natural disasters or force majeure.
  • Normal wear and tear (e.g., battery depletion, cosmetic scratches).

4. Claims Process

To initiate a warranty claim, please contact our support team at sztimmytech@gmail.com with your order number and proof of the defect (photos/videos). Our engineers will perform a remote diagnostic before authorizing a return or replacement.
